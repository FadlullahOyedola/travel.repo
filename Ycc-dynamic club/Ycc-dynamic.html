Frequently Asked Questions ❓

The Young Changemakers Club is open to all students passionate about community service and leadership development. We welcome students from all academic backgrounds and levels.

We organize a wide range of activities including donation drives, volunteer events at local orphanages and community centers, workshops on leadership and advocacy, and social justice campaigns. We also hold regular meetings for planning and discussion.

While our core members are students, we welcome support from the wider community! You can contribute through financial donations via our donation accounts, by volunteering your time, or by offering expertise for our workshops. Please reach out via our contact form!
