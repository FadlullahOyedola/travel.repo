About Our Institution

Our annual Islamic Camp is designed to provide a holistic environment for spiritual, intellectual, and physical development. We aim to instill Islamic values, foster brotherhood/sisterhood, and create lasting memories.

Participants will engage in a variety of activities including Quranic studies, Hadith lessons, interactive workshops, outdoor sports, team-building exercises, and inspiring lectures from renowned scholars.

The camp promotes a safe and nurturing atmosphere where individuals can deepen their understanding of Islam and connect with fellow Muslims in a positive setting.
