About GTBank

History & Evolution

GTBank, established in Nigeria in 1990, began operations in February 1991. It rapidly ascended to become a leading financial institution, now operating under the umbrella of Guaranty Trust Holding Company Plc (GTCO) since its transition in 2021. This strategic move has enabled GTCO to diversify its offerings beyond conventional banking.

A trailblazer in the Nigerian banking sector, GTBank holds distinctions such as being an early adopter of online banking and the first Nigerian bank to list on the prestigious London Stock Exchange in 2007, marking a significant milestone in its international presence.

Core Values & Recognition

GTBank is renowned for its unwavering commitment to:

  • Exceptional Customer Service: Placing the client experience at the forefront of its operations.
  • Continuous Innovation: Consistently introducing cutting-edge digital banking solutions.
  • Ethical Principles: Adhering strictly to "The Orange Rules," which champion simplicity, professionalism, and unwavering trustworthiness.
  • Industry Leadership: Evidenced by numerous prestigious national and international awards for its robust financial performance, innovative solutions, and strong corporate governance.
