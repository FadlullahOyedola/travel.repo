About FCMB Group Plc

FCMB Group Plc is a leading financial services institution in Nigeria, with a rich history dating back to 1977. We are a bank-led financial holding company dedicated to providing comprehensive financial solutions across banking, investment management, and investment banking.

With over 9 million customers and 204 branches in Nigeria, plus a banking subsidiary in the UK, we are committed to fostering economic growth and empowering individuals and businesses.

Key Facts About FCMB

  • Founded: Our roots trace back to City Securities Limited, established in September 1977.
  • Pioneering Spirit: First City Merchant Bank Limited (our precursor) was established in August 1983 as **Nigeria's first privately owned merchant bank** without government support.
  • Comprehensive Offerings: We operate through three main business groups: **Banking, Investment Management, and Investment Banking.**
  • Extensive Reach: Over **9 million customers** and **204 branches** in Nigeria, plus a banking subsidiary in the United Kingdom (FCMB Bank (UK) Limited).
  • Stock Exchange Listing: FCMB Group Plc is listed on the **Nigerian Stock Exchange (NSE)** with the ticker symbol 'FCMB'.
  • Growth through Acquisition: We've expanded through strategic mergers, including FinBank Plc in 2012, strengthening our market position.
  • Digital Focus: Significant growth in digital payments, lending, and wealth management, contributing notably to gross earnings.
  • Special Focus Areas: Strong commitment to supporting **SMEs, women-owned businesses, youth, and renewable energy** projects.

Our Achievements & Impact

FCMB's dedication to excellence, innovation, and community development has earned us numerous accolades and a strong reputation in the financial sector.

  • Consistent Profitability: Demonstrated strong financial results, with significant growth in gross earnings and profit after tax. For instance, in **2024, gross earnings grew by 53.9% to N794.8 billion**, and **profit after tax increased by 16.05% to N107.9 billion.**
  • Leading SME Support: Consistently recognized as **"Best SME Bank in Africa"** multiple times (e.g., 2020, 2021, 2022, 2024), and awarded for **"Highest Impact on MSMEs Accessing Credit for the First Time in Nigeria."**
  • Massive SME Lending: Disbursed **N180 billion in loans to over 800,000 MSMEs** between January and June 2024 alone, empowering small businesses across Nigeria.
  • Pioneering Digital Transformation: Achieved **69.2% growth in digital revenues** in 2024, with **1.6 million retail loans** and **18,000+ SME loans** disbursed through digital channels.
  • Empowering Women: Through **"SheVentures,"** provided over **N20 billion in interest-free loans** to women-owned SMEs in 2023, coupled with training and mentoring.
  • Sustainability Champion: Recognized for **"Highest Disbursement to Sustainability Projects,"** actively financing renewable energy initiatives and supporting projects that align with sustainable development goals.
  • Successful Capital Raising: Successfully raised **N144.6 billion** through a public offer in 2024, significantly strengthening our capital base.
  • Social Impact Initiatives: Invested in education, youth empowerment, and health initiatives, including providing scholarships and partnering to restore sight for over 2,000 people.
  • Human Resource Excellence: Received the ITF Merit Award as **"Best Contributing Employer in Human Resource Development"** for robust employee capacity development programs.
