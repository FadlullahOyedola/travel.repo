About Our Camp

Our annual Islamic Camp is designed to provide a holistic environment for spiritual, intellectual, and physical development. We aim to instill Islamic values, foster brotherhood/sisterhood, and create lasting memories.

Participants will engage in a variety of activities including Quranic studies, Hadith lessons, interactive workshops, outdoor sports, team-building exercises, and inspiring lectures from renowned scholars.

The camp promotes a safe and nurturing atmosphere where individuals can deepen their understanding of Islam and connect with fellow Muslims in a positive setting.

Camp Categories

Children's Program (Ages 6-12)

Our children's program focuses on foundational Islamic knowledge, character building, and engaging activities tailored for younger minds.

  • Basic Quranic recitation & memorization
  • Stories of the Prophets & Sahabah
  • Fun Islamic crafts & games
  • Outdoor play & sports
  • Interactive moral lessons

Adult Program (Ages 13+)

The adult program offers deeper insights into Islamic sciences, contemporary issues, and personal development.

  • Fiqh & Aqeedah sessions
  • Seerah & Tafsir studies
  • Marriage & family workshops
  • Career development & leadership talks
  • Q&A sessions with scholars
