3 Key Ingredients of MILO®

Malt

Malt is a nourishing source of energy. Nestle uses a unique extraction process to capture all the natural goodness from Sorghum malt in the production of MILO®.

Milk

Milk gives MILO® its creamy taste. It also provides protein and calcium that is important for kid's growth.​
