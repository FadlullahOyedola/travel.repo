DAURA 1446AH/2025

Daura (Islamic Training Program) remains a pillar of knowledge and spiritual growth for Muslims. Held annually each December, this 5–7 day event strengthens faith and unity.

Key Program Elements

  • Islamic Lectures: Enlightening talks from renowned scholars.
  • Tafsir Sessions: Deep dives into the Quran’s meaning.
  • Spiritual Nourishment: Faith workshops & activities.
  • Career Guidance: Align work with Islamic values.
  • Intellectual Discourse: Address modern issues through Islam.
  • Skill Development: Learn practical life skills.
  • Dawah Outreach: Hands-on outreach & training.
  • Community Cohesion: Build unity, brotherhood & sisterhood.
